About

I’m Alex, a high school student who’s passionate about using technology to make a real difference. I built MedLingual because I saw how hard it can be for non-English speakers and seniors with low vision to understand medication labels. Some of my own family members struggled with this, and I wanted to help.

I designed and developed MedLingual from scratch using React Native, Javascript, and some APIs. I hope this app can improve medication safety for many people and show how students like me can build tools that matter.
