App

MedLingual supports English, Spanish, Chinese, Tagalog, and Hindi—the most spoken languages in my district—enabling users to take or upload a photo of their medication. Using AI and OpenFDA, it identifies the medication and provides translated usage and warnings into the user’s preferred language.

I was inspired by family members and community members who find OTC medication labels hard to understand due to language barriers. MedLingual bridges that gap by providing clear, translated instructions.

An important feature I added is adjustable font sizes: as my mom's vision declined, reading small text became difficult. Now users can enlarge text for better readability.
