How the App Works

MedLingual makes it easy to understand over-the-counter medications. Just take a photo of your medicine, and the app does the rest.

Steps:

  • Take or upload a photo of the medication
  • The app uses AI to recognize the medicine name
  • It pulls trusted info from the OpenFDA database
  • Translates it into your selected language
  • Displays results in large, clean, readable text

Supported languages: English, Spanish, Chinese, Tagalog, Vietnamese, and Hindi.
