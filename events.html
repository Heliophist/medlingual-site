I’m also organizing events with local communities to promote MedLingual and help more people access it. My goal is to raise awareness about language and accessibility barriers in medication safety, and show how technology can help bridge that gap.

At these events, I’ll keep demonstrating how the app works, help users get started, and gather feedback for future improvements. I hope MedLingual empowers non-English speakers and seniors to safely understand and use their over-the-counter medications.
