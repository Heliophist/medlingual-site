Events

We are organizing events in partnership with local communities to promote MedLingual and help more people access it. Our goal is to raise awareness about language and accessibility barriers in medication safety, and show how technology can bridge that gap.

At these events, we demonstrate how the app works, help users get started, and gather feedback for future improvements. We hope MedLingual empowers non-English speakers and seniors to safely understand and use their over-the-counter medications.
