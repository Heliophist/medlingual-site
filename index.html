Welcome to MedLingual - YOUR Medicine, YOUR Language

MedLingual is an AI-powered mobile app we designed to help people better understand their over-the-counter (OTC) medications, especially if they don’t speak English or have limited vision. Using artificial intelligence, the app identifies the medication from a photo and instantly pulls trusted, FDA-approved information, like usage, dosage, side effects, and warnings. That information is then translated into one of six supported languages and displayed in large, readable text.

This project started because we saw how easy it is for language barriers and unclear labels to cause serious confusion. Our goal with MedLingual is to make medication information accessible, accurate, and safe for everyone.
