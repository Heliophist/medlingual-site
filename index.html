Welcome to MedLingual

MedLingual is an AI-powered mobile app I designed to help people better understand their over-the-counter (OTC) medications, especially if they don’t speak English or have limited vision. Using artificial intelligence, the app identifies the medication from a photo and instantly pulls trusted, FDA-approved information—like usage, dosage, side effects, and warnings. That information is then translated into one of six supported languages and displayed in large, readable text.

This project started because I saw how easy it is for language barriers and unclear labels to cause serious confusion. My goal with MedLingual is to make medication information accessible, accurate, and safe for everyone.

Key Features

  • Photo Recognition – Snap or upload a picture of any OTC drug
  • Reliable Information – Pulled directly from the OpenFDA database
  • Language Support – English, Spanish, Chinese, Tagalog, Vietnamese, and Hindi
  • Accessibility First – Adjustable fonts and clean layout for low-vision users
  • Smart AI Design – Choose between high-accuracy or cost-efficient versions
